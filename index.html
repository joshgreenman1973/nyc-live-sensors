Methodology

About this page

  • This dashboard was built with AI assistance. Every number is fetched live from the public feed named on its tile — nothing is stored, modeled or adjusted by this page, with one labeled exception: the air-quality tile is a model estimate, not a physical sensor, and is marked as such.
  • Feeds go down, lag or report bad values; each tile shows its own "as of" time and goes gray when its feed is stale or unreachable.
  • Readings here are for curiosity, not safety decisions. For emergencies rely on official channels, not this page.
  • Spot-check anything you plan to rely on. See the methodology for every endpoint, refresh cadence and known caveat.
